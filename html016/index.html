Musa acuminata
Musa acuminata is a species of banana native to Southern Asia, its range comprising the Indian Subcontinent and Southeast Asia. Many of the modern edible dessert bananas are from this species, although some are hybrids with Musa balbisiana.
Malus domestica
The nutrients in apples make this fruit a true delight for health. Known worldwide for its delicious flavor and crunchy texture, the apple has a rich history dating back thousands of years in Central Asia.
The Pineapple
Pineapple is a delicious, healthy, and very popular fruit in Brazil. It is also part of the prestigious list of superfoods. This term is relatively new here, however, researchers and nutrition professionals consider some fruits to be so beneficial and functional for human health that they deserve this classification.
